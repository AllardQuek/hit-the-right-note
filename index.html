Hit The Right Notes

Always wanted to play the piano but never know what notes to hit? It's simple.

1. Use the a-f and j-; keys on your keyboard or touch the coloured blocks to play the piano.

2. Use the space bar to control the sustain pedal.

3. The more you pretend you're a real player, the better the melody (and you!) will sound.

Built with magenta.js. Learn more about how it works, or see the code on Glitch.
