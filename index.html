Piano Genie

Have some fun pretending you're a piano virtuoso using machine learning!
Use the 1-8 numbered keys on your keyboard, or touch the coloured blocks to play the piano. The more you pretend you're a real player, the better the melody, and you, will sound!

Please put for phone in landscape mode.
